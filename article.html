Collision Repair Equipment

We utilize state of the art computerized frame measuring systems to restore vehicles back to factory specifications. We perform MIG welds, silicon bronze welds, and aluminum welds to name a few. We have capability to match factory spot welds to duplicate an OEM factory finish. When it comes to plastic bumpers and other accessories, we utilize a nitrogen welder that allows us to expertly perform plastic repairs. Multiple In ground and post lifts allow us to inspect and perform mechanical repairs.

Auto Refinishing & Painting

We use heated downdraft spray booths to ensure a flawless/durable finish. In addition to this we have built an extensive library of difficult color variations achieving true color match.
